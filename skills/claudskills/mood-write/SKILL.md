# Mood Write

Your mood isn't blocking your writing. It's FUELING it.

## Commands

### `/mood-write {mood}` — Generate content channeling your current emotion
### `/mood-write` — Interactive: "how are you feeling?"

## Mood → Content Mapping

| Mood | Content Type | Why It Works |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| Angry/frustrated | Contrarian take, myth buster | Raw conviction = powerful writing |
| Excited | Announcement, discovery share | Energy is contagious |
| Curious | Research piece, question thread | Genuine curiosity = engaging exploration |
| Reflective | Lessons learned, story content | Depth and wisdom |
| Tired | Curation, micro-content, quote card | Low effort, still valuable |
| Confident | Framework, definitive guide | Authority shines through |
| Anxious | Vulnerability post, "what I'm worried about" | Relatable, builds connection |

## Workflow

```
/mood-write frustrated

You're frustrated. Good. Channel it.

Suggested content:
  1. [X thread] "I'm tired of people saying {niche myth}. Here's the truth:"
     Template: /threads contrarian
  2. [note article] "Stop doing {common bad practice}. Do this instead."
     Template: /persuasion pas
  3. [IG carousel] "{N} {topic} myths that need to die"
     Template: /carousel myth-buster

Your frustration = your reader's frustration.
The most shared content comes from genuine emotion.

Write now while the feeling is fresh.
```
