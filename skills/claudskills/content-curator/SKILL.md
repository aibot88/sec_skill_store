# Content Curator

Creating is hard. Curating is smart. Share others' brilliance with YOUR perspective.

## When to Activate

- User says `/curate` or `/curate {topic}`
- User asks "find good content to share"
- User says "what should I share this week?"

## Commands

### `/curate` — Find and curate content for this week
### `/curate {topic}` — Curate on specific topic
### `/curate thread` — Generate "what I read this week" thread
### `/curate newsletter` — Generate curated newsletter edition

## Curation Formats

| Format | Platform | Effort | Authority |
|--------|----------|--------|-----------|
| Quote-tweet + comment | X | Very Low | Medium |
| "5 things I read" thread | X | Low | High |
| Curated newsletter | Email | Medium | Very High |
| "Weekly links" note article | note | Medium | High |
| Story reshare + commentary | Instagram | Low | Medium |

## Workflow

### Step 1: Find Content Worth Sharing

WebSearch for high-quality content in your niche:
```
Search: "{niche} best articles this week"
Search: "{keyword}" — sort by recent, high quality
Search: Leading voices in {niche} — latest posts
```

Filter criteria:
- Genuinely valuable (not self-promotional)
- From credible sources
- Your audience would benefit
- You can add unique commentary

### Step 2: Add Your Expert Commentary

For each curated piece, generate YOUR take:

```
============================================
  Weekly Curation: {N} pieces selected
============================================

1. "{title}" by {author}
   URL: {url}
   Your commentary: "{what makes this valuable + your unique angle}"
   Format: Quote-tweet with analysis

2. "{title}" by {author}
   URL: {url}
   Your commentary: "{agree/disagree + why + what your audience should take away}"
   Format: Thread mention

3. "{title}" by {author}
   ...

--- "What I Read This Week" Thread ---

Tweet 1: "{N} things I read this week that changed how I think about {topic}:"

Tweet 2: "1/ {title} by {author}
  My takeaway: {your unique insight}
  → {url}"

Tweet 3: "2/ {title} by {author}
  My takeaway: {insight}
  → {url}"
...

Last tweet: "What did YOU read this week? Drop your best link below."

============================================
```

### Step 3: Curation Calendar

```
Curation schedule:
  Monday: Quote-tweet 1 great article
  Wednesday: Share 1 article with long commentary
  Friday: "What I read this week" thread (5 items)
  Sunday: Curated newsletter to email list
```

## Quality Gate

- [ ] Curated content is genuinely high quality
- [ ] YOUR commentary adds unique value (not just "great article!")
- [ ] Proper attribution to original creators
- [ ] Mix of sources (not always the same people)
- [ ] Curation supports your identity/positioning
